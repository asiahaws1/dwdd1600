Our Authentication Process & Promise

Authentication Process
We are extremely dedicated to the cause of finding vintage clothes. When searching for vintage clothes, there are many rare and unique items that may have influenced pop-culture. These items can be seen as collectibles to individuals dedicated to fashion. Rare vintage fashion pieces can be a piece of history, and can be found anywhere. We are passionate about picking items that are timeless, while also upcoming. Rare finds can be charactarized in many ways, and we are here to help! Our authentication experts cross-reference and thoroughly analyze each vintage piece before shipping. We check production dates, tags, stitching, and more to fully authenticate our items.
Our Sourcing
Our pieces are picked from various places, from online pieces to unique shops, flea markerts, vintage collectors,and everything in between. We analyze and hand-pick pieces that are appealing, upcoming, exciting and trendy. All of the pieces in our inventory are authenticated as soon as we source them, and before they are listed for purchase.
Our Promise
Our Promise to you is that all items recieved are 100% real and authentic vintage. We make sure our products meet high-quality standards. All of our vintage products are free of hard-wear, tears, excessive fading, holes, loose stitching, and cracks. We do not buy or sell damaged vintage items. We make sure our items are ready for you to wear!
